"The Elevate™ Yellow Egg Spatula is a stylish and functional kitchen tool designed specifically for handling eggs with precision and ease. PRODUCT DETAILS


Precision Design for Eggs: The Elevate™ Yellow Egg Spatula is uniquely crafted with a precision edge, making it ideal for flipping, turning, and serving eggs. The thin, flexible blade ensures gentle handling, preventing damage to delicate egg whites and yolks.
High-Quality Materials: Crafted from premium materials, the spatula combines a durable stainless-steel blade with a heat-resistant, food-grade silicone coating. This construction ensures both longevity and safety, making the spatula suitable for use in high-heat cooking environments.
Elevating Feature: The spatula is part of the Elevate™ series and features an integrated tool rest. This built-in feature lifts the spatula's head off the counter or stovetop, preventing cross-contamination and maintaining hygiene during cooking.
